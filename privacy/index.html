Privacy Policy

Continuum AI Advisor

Effective date: April 26, 2026

Overview

Continuum AI Advisor ("Continuum", "we", "us") helps users capture voice and text input, retrieve saved context, and receive AI-generated assistance. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as email and authentication identifiers).
  • User content you provide, including messages, notes, and optional voice input.
  • Device and usage data needed for app reliability and security.
  • Approximate or precise location only when you grant permission.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve core app features, including AI responses and memory retrieval.
  • Maintain account security, prevent abuse, and diagnose technical issues.
  • Support customer requests and app review troubleshooting.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Sharing

We may share limited data with service providers who help operate the app (for example, hosting, analytics, or AI processing infrastructure). We do not sell personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as needed for app functionality, legal compliance, and security. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.

Your Choices

  • You can control app permissions (microphone, speech recognition, photos, camera, location) in iOS Settings.
  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your data.

Contact

For privacy or data requests, email support@continuumadvisor.ai.
